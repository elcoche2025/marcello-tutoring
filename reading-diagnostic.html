Diagnostic Assessment Overview

This assessment mirrors Amplify mCLASS / DIBELS-style screening. It evaluates foundational reading skills across four areas aligned to CCSS RF.3.3 and RF.3.4. Administer sections in order. Each section has a printable student copy.

Section Standard What It Measures Time
ORF Passages (x3) RF.3.4.a/b/c Fluency rate (WCPM), accuracy, prosody, self-correction 1 min each
Multisyllabic Words RF.3.3.c Decoding 2-4 syllable words in isolation ~2 min
Prefixes & Suffixes RF.3.3.a/b Recognizing and reading words with affixes ~2 min
Irregular Words RF.3.3.d Reading irregularly spelled grade-level words ~1 min

How to Administer

  1. Print the student copies — click "Print Student Copy" on each section. These are clean, large-font versions for Marcello to read from.
  2. ORF Passages: Have Marcello read aloud for 1 minute. On your screen, click words he misreads (errors) or self-corrects. When the timer beeps, click the last word he read.
  3. Word Lists: Show him the printed word list. Click each word as correct or incorrect on your screen as he reads.
  4. Use median ORF score — administer all 3 passages, take the middle score as his WCPM.
