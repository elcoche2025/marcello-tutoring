🌊 Jacques Cousteau 🌊

An Easy-to-Read Summary

📌 Main Idea

Jacques Cousteau loved the ocean. He explored it and made movies so everyone could see how beautiful it is.

1

Jacques was sick as a child. Doctors told him to swim to get stronger. He loved swimming!

2

He loved to build things. He liked to make gadgets and cameras.

3

He got hurt in a car accident. Swimming in the ocean helped him get better.

4

Someone gave him goggles. When he looked underwater, he saw an amazing world!

5

He invented the Aqua-Lung. This was a special tank that let people breathe underwater for a long time.

6

He made underwater cameras. He made movies so people could see the ocean too.

7

He bought a boat called the Calypso. He sailed all over the world to explore.

8

He made a movie called "The Silent World." It was the first big movie about underwater life. Everyone loved it!

9

He had a TV show. It showed whales, dolphins, and octopuses. People watched it in their homes.

10

He saw the ocean was getting dirty. He started a group to protect the ocean and keep it clean.

⭐ Important Words to Know

  • Aqua-Lung: A tank that lets people breathe underwater
  • Explorer: Someone who goes to new places to learn about them
  • Calypso: The name of Jacques Cousteau's boat
  • Pollution: When the water or air gets dirty
  • Protect: To keep something safe

💡 Why is Jacques Cousteau Important?

He helped people see and love the ocean. He taught us to take care of the water and the animals that live in it.
