Goal: Write 2-3 sentences of Essay 5 using the new approach.

The Protocol: Tell Me → Find It → Write It

1
TELL ME (Oral, 2-3 min)

"Tell me about your reason. Just talk." Tutor types EXACTLY what Marcello says verbatim. Do not interrupt.

2
FIND THE SENTENCE (1-2 min)

Show his words on screen. "Can you find ONE sentence hiding in here? Point to the part that would make a good sentence."

3
BUILD IT (Labeled Frames) (2-3 min)

Use specific labeled blanks, not open ones.

"I think schools should [have] ___[what]___ because [kids] ___[what happens]___."
4
CHECK (30 sec)

Read it back with speechSynthesis. "Does that sound right?"

Differentiation & Troubleshooting Strategies
If short response in "Tell Me":

Ask: "What does that look like?", "If I was at your school, what would I SEE?", "Pretend you're the teacher."

If he goes on a long monologue:

Let him finish! Then say: "That was AMAZING. Now let's find the ONE sentence hiding in that story."

If labeled frames are too hard:

Pre-fill more. Offer 3 options based on what he said. Last resort: you write from his dictation, he confirms.
