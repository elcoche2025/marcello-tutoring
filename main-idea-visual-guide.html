🎯 How to Find the MAIN IDEA 🎯

A Visual Guide for Marcello

1
Ask Yourself the BIG Question
🤔 If you could only tell ONE thing
about this story, what would it be?

Imagine your friend wasn't here today. They ask: "What was the story about?" You only have 10 SECONDS to answer. What would you say? That's probably the MAIN IDEA! 💡

2
Use the Magic Formula
Main Idea = 2 Parts
WHO or WHAT
+
THE MOST IMPORTANT THING ABOUT IT
1
WHAT is it about?
Answer: Sharks / Shark attacks
2
What ABOUT them?
Answer: They're very rare / Not really dangerous
3
Put it together!
"Shark attacks are very rare and people can stay safe."
3
The Umbrella Trick
☂️
The Main Idea is like a BIG UMBRELLA. It covers ALL the details!
MAIN IDEA
Shark attacks are rare and sharks don't want to eat people
↓ ↓ ↓
Detail 1:
Less than ~100 attacks per year
Detail 2:
Sharks eat fish and seals
Detail 3:
Ways to stay safe
✅ Good Main Ideas vs ❌ Bad Main Ideas
TOO SMALL: "Sharks have sharp teeth." This is just ONE detail, not the whole story.
TOO VAGUE: "This story is about sharks." That's the topic; what's the most important thing?
TOO SPECIFIC: "You should swim in groups." A safety tip, not the big idea.
JUST RIGHT: "Shark attacks are very rare and people can stay safe by following rules."
ALSO GOOD: "Sharks don't want to eat people and attacks don't happen very often."
4
Look for Repeating Ideas

What words or ideas show up AGAIN and AGAIN in the story?

In the Shark Story, these ideas repeat:
🔄 "Rare" — mentioned multiple times
🔄 "Sharks don't want to eat people" — said in different ways
🔄 "Stay safe" — safety tips throughout
🦈 Let's Practice with the Shark Story! 🦈
Follow These Steps:
1
What is the story mostly about?
"Shark attacks" or "sharks and people"
2
What's the most important thing about shark attacks?
"They're very rare" and "sharks don't want to eat us"
3
Does this cover the WHOLE story?
✅ Yes! The story talks about how rare attacks are, why they happen, and how to stay safe.
✓
MAIN IDEA:
"Shark attacks are very rare and sharks don't really want to eat people, but there are ways to stay safe."
